A place where talents are natured and grown

Talanta Musical and Computer Academy is a registered training centre located in western part of Kenya within Bungoma County. It is located at Lugulu along Webuye Kitale road next to Lugulu mission Hospital within the Friends Church compound. Our mandate is to meet the needs of the society in nurturing and growing talents across Kenya and help youths identify their potential and make informed quality choices in their lives.Talanta musical and Computer academy boasts of the following:

  • • Its serene learning environment free from disturbances and external noises, making it conducive for students’ concentration.
  • • Well-equipped music and computer labs
  • • Trained professional tutors with great experience
  • • Well-equipped recording studios that makes practical’s more interesting
  • • A one on one student teacher coaching interaction.
Talanta specializes in nurturing and growing talents across various age groups in Kenya, especially youths. We also look forward to improving praise and worship in church groups, bands, individual artists, schools etc. We as well offer all computer packages at a very friendly fee to our trainees.Proudly Talanta,we grow talents!.
